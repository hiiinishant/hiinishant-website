There's something magical about studying at 2 AM. The notifications stop. The noise dies down. And for the first time all day, your brain has space to actually think.

I didn't plan to build an entire education platform around this idea. It started organically — I'd study late, take notes, and share them with friends who were also up. Before I knew it, hundreds of students were waiting for my next upload.

The name **2 AM Study** wasn't a marketing gimmick. It was an honest reflection of when the best learning happened for me and thousands of students like me.

If you're a night owl student feeling guilty about your schedule — don't. Find your peak hours and protect them fiercely. That's where your breakthroughs live.
