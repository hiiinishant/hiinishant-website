It started in a college dorm room. I was struggling with the same problem every student faces — too much content, too little structure, and no one to guide the way.

So I started making my own notes. Clean, concise, organized. Friends asked for copies. Then friends of friends. Then strangers on the internet.

By 2022, the demand was undeniable. **2 AM Study** launched as a proper platform — courses, a store, a community. Not because I had a business plan, but because students needed it to exist.

Today, 100K+ students use what we build. But the core mission hasn't changed: make learning accessible, engaging, and effective for everyone who refuses to give up.
